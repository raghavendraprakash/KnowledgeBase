Here are the sessions related to "Disaster Recovery" at AWS re:Invent 2025 with the requested details based on the official AWS blog about operations management and resilience:

1. Title: Automate disaster recovery playbooks using generative AI  
   Description: Learn how to strengthen your mission-critical workloads with automated disaster recovery planning using Amazon Q Developer, AWS Resilience Hub, and AWS Systems Manager. This session demonstrates practical techniques for generating and validating recovery runbooks with automated testing capabilities.  
   Level: Builders’ Session  
   Date and Time: Thursday, December 4, 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM PST  
   Target Audience: Cloud architects, DevOps engineers, IT professionals responsible for disaster recovery planning  
   Source URI: https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/mt/building-your-operations-management-with-ai-powered-operations-at-reinvent-2025/

2. Title: AI-powered resilience testing and disaster recovery  
   Description: Enhance resilience and disaster recovery on AWS empowered by AI. Learn to leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) with AWS Resilience Hub and AWS Systems Manager to modernize testing, analyze infrastructure, and generate AWS Fault Injection Service experiments plus recovery runbooks.  
   Level: Breakout Session (Expert-level)  
   Date and Time: Tuesday, December 2, 1:30 PM – 2:30 PM PST  
   Target Audience: Experienced cloud architects, engineers focused on high availability and disaster recovery  
   Source URI: https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/mt/building-your-operations-management-with-ai-powered-operations-at-reinvent-2025/

These sessions focus on innovative approaches for disaster recovery automation, AI-driven resilience testing, and operational strategies to ensure business continuity using AWS services.
